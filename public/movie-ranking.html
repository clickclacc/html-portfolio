The Best Movies According to Julie

Top 3 Movies of All-time.

Crouching Tiger, Hidden Dragon

Michelle Yeoh. It has stunning scenery and cute romance. The selling point here is also the badass fighting scenes and constant action. Good pacing. Strong female characters.

Spirited Away

Nothing can compare to the wimsy and stunning visuals of a Miyasaki film. There is something about the way he captures childhood perspective while simultaneously throwing his characters into complex and textured worlds.

Everything Everywhere All At Once

So cute. The resolve of the realtionship at the end and the mother accepting her daughter and becky. A middle-aged women getting to play a complex character. simultaneously being very deep and not taking itself too seriously
